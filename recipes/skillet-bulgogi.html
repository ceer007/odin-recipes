Skillet Bulgogi

Ready in 30 minutes. "This is a quick and easy, but very tasty meal," says daveparks4. "You can substitute the chicken with beef or pork for variety. My wife and I absolutely love this recipe, and it only takes 30 minutes with very few dirty dishes! Serve over rice."

Ingredients( 4 serving )

  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 ½ tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
